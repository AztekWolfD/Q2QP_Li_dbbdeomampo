Mission:

The Child Mind Institute is dedicated to transforming the lives of children and families struggling with mental health and learning disorders by providing comprehensive care, conducting groundbreaking research, and advocating for policies that prioritize children's mental wellness. Through their mission, they strive to ensure that every child and adolescent receives the support, resources, and understanding they need to thrive mentally, emotionally, and academically. By offering evidence-based care, innovative research initiatives, educational programs, and community advocacy efforts, the Institute aims to create a world where children's mental health is valued, understood, and addressed with compassion and expertise.

Vision:

The Child Mind Institute envisions a world where every child and adolescent facing mental health or learning challenges is met with understanding, compassion, and effective support. In this vision, children are empowered to navigate their emotions, learn, and grow without the barriers posed by untreated mental health conditions. It's a world where communities come together to prioritize mental wellness, ensuring that every young person has the opportunity to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society. This vision drives the Institute's unwavering commitment to providing innovative care, conducting groundbreaking research, and advocating for policies that prioritize the mental health and well-being of children and families everywhere.
